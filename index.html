Stock Market Data

GitHub: https://github.com/maanavshah/stock-market-india

API Routes/Endpoints

→ Check Market Status

URL: http://localhost:3000/get_market_status

National Stock Exchange(NSE)

→ Get all the indices of NSE(change, year high and low, index order)

URL: /nse/get_indices

→ Get the quotes of all indexes in NSE

URL: /nse/get_quotes

→ Get the quotation data of the symbol (companyName) from NSE

URL: /nse/get_quote_info?companyName=MARUTI

→ Get the quotation data of the symbols (companyNames) from NSE

URL: /nse/get_multiple_quote_info?companyNames=TCS,WIPRO,AND_MORE

→ Get the top 10 gainers of NSE

URL: /nse/get_gainers

→ Get the top 10 losers of NSE

URL: /nse/get_losers

→ Get advances/declines of individual index, and the value if its changed or not

URL: /nse/get_incline_decline

→ Get advances/declines of individual index, and the value if its changed or not

URL: /nse/get_index_stocks?symbol=nifty

→ Get the list of companies in provided NSE index with matching keyword data

URL: /nse/search_stocks?keyword=AXIS

→ Get the intraday data of company in NSE

URL: /nse/get_intra_day_data?companyName=TCS&time=1

URL: /nse/get_intra_day_data?companyName=TCS&time=month

→ Get 52 weeks all high stocks in NSE

URL: /nse/get_52_week_high

→ Get 52 weeks all low stocks in NSE

URL: /nse/get_52_week_low

→ Get the NSE stocks with highest values

URL: /nse/get_top_value_stocks

→ Get the NSE stocks with highest sold volumes

URL: /nse/get_top_volume_stocks

→ Get the futures data for a company stock (symbol) and time

URL: /nse/get_stock_futures_data?companyName=TCS&time=15

URL: /nse/get_stock_futures_data?companyName=VEDL&time=month

→ Get chart data of a company name(symbol) depending on time in NSE

URL: /nse/get_chart_data_new?companyName=VEDL&time=5

URL: /nse/get_chart_data_new?companyName=VEDL&time=year

Bombay Stock Exchange(BSE)

→ Get details of all index in BSE Stock exchange

URL: /bse/get_indices

→ Get all the indices of NSE(Get the information of only a single index

URL: /bse/get_indices

→ Get today's closing data and daily data of past time using IndexId and time from BSE

URL: /bse/get_index_chart_data?indexId=16

→ Gets the StockValue, Volume for company in specified past time
// 500112 - symbol (securityCode) of SBIN stock BSE

URL: /bse/get_company_info?companyKey=500112

→ Get the stocks chart data

URL: /bse/get_chart_data_new?companyName=VEDL&time=5

URL: /bse/get_chart_data_new?companyName=VEDL&time=year

→ Get BSE stock data of stock info and day chart

URL: /bse/get_stock_info_and_day_chart_data?companyKey=500325

→ Get the top gainers of BSE stock exchange

URL: /bse/get_gainers

→ Get the top losers of BSE stock exchange

URL: /bse/get_losers

→ Get the top turnovers of BSE stock exchange

URL: /bse/getTopTurnOvers
